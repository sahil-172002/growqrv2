GrowQR FAQ - Current Questions and Answers
==========================================

CATEGORY: OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Q1: What exactly is GrowQR?
A: GrowQR is the world's first AI-powered Skill Identity & Readiness platform. One lifetime QR Key links you forever to your dynamic Q-Score — a living, verifiable composite of 25+ readiness attributes (IQ, EQ, AQ, SQ, and more).

---

Q2: How is GrowQR different from Resumes or any other profile?
A: Unlike static profiles or generic Resumes, your single GrowQR is a permanent, evolving digital identity. It powers real-time Q-Score updates, personalized AI coaching, crypto rewards for upskilling, and trusted matchmaking across individuals, organizations, institutions, and governments.

---

Q3: How do I get my unique GrowQR right now?
A: The platform is in active development. Join the waitlist at growqr.ai — the earliest members will claim their lifetime QR first when we open private beta in Q1–Q2 2026.


================================================================================
CATEGORY: Q-SCORE
================================================================================

Q4: What is Q-Score?
A: Q-Score is your Cumulative Readiness Quotient — an AI-computed single score that aggregates 25+ human capability dimensions (Adaptability, Social Branding, Perception, Body Language, Efficiency, Technical, Emotional, etc.) into one trusted, evolving metric.

---

Q5: How does my Q-Score improve over time?
A: Through AI-powered assessments, micro-courses, real-world practice, and verified milestones. Your personal AI agent designs custom growth paths and rewards every step with badges and crypto.

---

Q6: Why is Q-Score more trusted than a resume or certificate?
A: It's dynamic, multi-dimensional, and continuously verified — employers, universities, and governments see your real readiness, not just what you claim.


================================================================================
CATEGORY: ECOSYSTEM
================================================================================

Q7: How does the entire GrowQR ecosystem work together?
A: One QR connects four pillars in real time: Individuals (grow & earn), Organizations (smart hiring), Institutions (curriculum & placement), and Governments/Smart Cities (citizen readiness & development programs).

---

Q8: What's in it for organizations and universities?
A: Instant access to pre-verified talent via Q-Score filters, talent intelligence dashboards, industry-aligned training insights, and dramatically better hiring/placement outcomes.

---

Q9: Can governments and smart cities really use GrowQR?
A: Yes — they use population-level Q-Score insights to design targeted upskilling programs, measure community readiness, and connect citizens to relevant jobs and opportunities at scale.


================================================================================
CATEGORY: AUDIENCE
================================================================================

Q10: Who can join GrowQR?
A: Everyone: Individuals (professionals, students, career changers), Organizations (companies of any size), Institutions (universities, training providers), and Governments/Smart Cities.

---

Q11: As an individual, what do I actually gain?
A: Your lifelong QR, a trusted Q-Score, a personal AI coach, crypto rewards for learning, and direct connections to verified roles and gigs that match your exact readiness.

---

Q12: Do organizations and institutions pay differently?
A: Yes — individuals start free with limited access and pay for premium features. Organizations, universities, and government partners will have tailored enterprise plans (details released closer to launch).
